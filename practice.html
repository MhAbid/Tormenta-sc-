Rules and Regulatioins

  • Every club member has to come to the field at least 3-4 days a week. In case any member is unable to do that then he has to give solid reasones for his absence. Otherwise,he will be penalized.
  • Every member has to give a monthly fee between the dates of 1 to 10 of every month. The monthly fee is 100 taka.
  • If any member is unable to clear his monthly donations by the given time then there will be a late fee of taka 10 every extra five days.
  • Each and every member has to obey the orders of the team menager and the captain.
  • The disicion of the manager and the senior ones will be the final desicion and everyone has to obey that.
  • Every player should maintain there position in the game.
  • Every member has to respond in the group conversations and should give their opinions.
  • Every member has to give respect to each others.
  • No one is allowed to use slang or bad words.
  • Violations and bad behavior are not allowed.
  • Each and everyone has to follow all of the rules and regulations.
Note: If ther is anyone who does not want to follow rules and regulations given above then he has to leave the club.
          If anyone is noticed breaking the rules then he will be punished or will be banned from the club forever.
